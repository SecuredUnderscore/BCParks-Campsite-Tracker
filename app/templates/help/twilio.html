Setting up Twilio SMS

Twilio allows you to send SMS notifications and verify phone numbers.

Step 1: Get Credentials

  1. Log in to Twilio Console.
  2. On the dashboard, find your Account SID (starts with AC) and Auth Token.
  3. Get a Twilio Phone Number (or buy one) to use as the "From Number".

Step 2: Create Verify Service

  1. Go to Verify > Services in the sidebar.
  2. Create a New Service (e.g. "Campsite Alert").
  3. Copy the Service SID (starts with VA...).

Step 3: Admin Configuration

  • Account SID: Your AC... SID
  • Auth Token: Your secret token
  • Verify Service SID: Your VA... SID
  • From Number: Your Twilio number (e.g. +1234567890)
