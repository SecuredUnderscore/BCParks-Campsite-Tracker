Setting up SMTP (Free Cloudflare/Gmail Method)

To send emails from your custom domain (e.g., alerts@yourdomain.com) without paying for enterprise email services, you can use Gmail's free SMTP server as a relay.

Step 1: Receive Emails (Cloudflare)

  1. Go to Cloudflare Dashboard > Email Routing.
  2. Create a custom address (e.g., alerts@...) routing to your personal Gmail.
  3. Verify the address in Gmail and add the required DNS records in Cloudflare.

Step 2: Get Google App Password

  1. Go to Google Security.
  2. Enable 2-Step Verification.
  3. Search for "App Passwords". Create one named "Campsite Tracker".
  4. Copy the 16-character code. This is your SMTP Password.

Step 3: Admin Configuration

  • Provider: SMTP
  • Host: smtp.gmail.com
  • Port: 587
  • Username: Your real Gmail address (e.g., john@gmail.com)
  • Password: The 16-char App Password
  • From Email: Your custom domain (e.g., alerts@yourdomain.com)
